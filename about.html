Our Story

BORN FROM PASSION FOR THE BEAUTIFUL GAME

KickZone started in 2025 from a simple idea: every football fan deserves access to authentic, high-quality gear at fair prices. What began as a small market stall in Gaborone has grown into one of the Botswana's most trusted online football stores.

We work directly with official manufacturers to bring you genuine shirts and boots — no replicas, no compromises. Whether you're a Sunday league striker or a die-hard supporter, we've got the kit for you!

What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

✅
100% Authenticity

Every product we sell is sourced directly from official brand distributors and licensed manufacturers. We guarantee authenticity or your money back.

🏆
Quality First

We rigorously test every product before listing it. From shirt stitching to boot stud grip — if it's not the best, it doesn't make our shelves.

🌱
Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint. Our packaging is 100% recyclable and we offset carbon from every delivery.

💬
Customer Care

Our support team are passionate football fans who understand what you need. We're available 7 days a week via chat, email, and phone.

💰
Fair Pricing

We believe premium football gear shouldn't break the bank. We price competitively and offer regular deals, loyalty rewards, and student discounts.

⚡
Fast Delivery

Order before 2pm for same-day dispatch. Standard delivery in 2–3 days, express next-day available. Free delivery on orders over P50.
