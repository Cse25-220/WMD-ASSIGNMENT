Mbappé Set to Join Real Madrid on a 5-Year Deal

Published on 23 May 2024

MADRID — The football world has shifted on its axis as Kylian Mbappé has officially agreed to personal terms with Spanish giants Real Madrid. Following months of intense media speculation, the French national captain will make his highly anticipated move to the Santiago Bernabéu on a free transfer this summer.

Sources close to the club indicate that Mbappé will sign a lucrative five-year contract, inheriting the iconic number 9 jersey previously held by Karim Benzema. The addition of the 25-year-old superstar strengthens an already frightening attacking frontline featuring Vinícius Júnior and Jude Bellingham.

An official club presentation is planned immediately following the conclusion of the UEFA Champions League Final, where fans are expected to fill the stadium to witness his historic unveiling.
