Ibrahim Mazza on a transfer to English Giant Manchester City

Published on 23 May 2026

MUNCHEN — Manchester City has been linked with a potential transfer for the 21-year-old algerian international as a replacement for benardo silva.

An offer of 45 million euros has been to Bayer Leverkusen with add on amount to the total of 55 million euros.

Manchester City’s pursuit of Ibrahim Maza is heating up fast — and with Pep Guardiola reportedly a huge admirer of the Algerian wonderkid, this could be one of the transfer stories to watch all summer long.
