Osmane Dembélé Sidelined with Severe Knee Injury

Published on 23 May 2024

PARI S — PSG has confirmed the shocking news that Ousmane Dembélé will be sidelined for an extended period due to a severe knee injury sustained during a recent training session.

The 25-year-old French international is expected to miss several weeks of action, casting uncertainty over his availability for the upcoming UEFA Champions League campaign.

IT is uncertain whether he will be able to return in time for the start of the next season or for the UCL final.
