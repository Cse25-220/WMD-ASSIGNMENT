Aston Villa Crowned Europa League Champions

Published on 23 May 2026

BILBAO — Aston Villa have officially been crowned UEFA Europa League champions after a stunning victory over Tottenham Hotspur in the final.

Unai Emery’s side produced a brilliant performance under the lights, with Villa supporters celebrating one of the greatest nights in the club’s modern history as they lifted a major European trophy once again.

The triumph marks a historic moment for Aston Villa and secures Champions League football next season, capping off an unforgettable European campaign.
