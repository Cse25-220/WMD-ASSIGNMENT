Vini Jr. on a transfer to English Giant Manchester City

Published on 23 May 2026

MUNCHEN — Manchester City has been linked with a potential transfer for the 24-year-old brasil international as a winger option after a fallout with his current club.

An offer of 145 million euros has been to Real Madrid with add on amount to the total of 195 million euros, breaking the previous transfer record of liverpool signing Florian Writz on 125 million euros deal.

Manchester City’s pursuit of Vini Jr. is heating up fast — and with Pep Guardiola reportedly a huge admirer of the Brasil winger, this could be one of the transfer stories to watch all summer long.
